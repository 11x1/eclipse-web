What is Eclipse?

Eclipse was made aiming to provide a high quality script for everyone, for free. Currently we offer primordial, neverlose and gamesense scripts for hundreds of users.

We strive for greateness, that is why we offer new features with a lot of customization options. Experience new, truly unique and revolutionizing indicator designs, an in-depth antiaim builder and many other cool additions, with clear labels that make it easy to understand what each feature does.

You are also able to share your script configs with other community members in our Discord server.
